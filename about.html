About PEGASUS

Our Story

PEGASUS was founded in 2020 with a mission to revolutionize the sports streaming experience. We saw a gap in the market for a platform that offers comprehensive coverage of all major sports leagues and events in one place, with a user-friendly interface and premium features.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of sports fans worldwide, providing live coverage of over 50 sports, including football, basketball, tennis, Formula 1, MMA, and more. Our platform features:

  • Live streaming of major sporting events
  • On-demand replays and highlights
  • Exclusive content and analysis
  • 24/7 dedicated sports channels
  • Multi-device compatibility

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate sports fans and technology experts working together to deliver the best streaming experience. With backgrounds from leading tech companies and sports media organizations, we're committed to innovation and excellence.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading sports streaming platform, connecting fans with their favorite sports anytime, anywhere, on any device. We're constantly adding new features and expanding our coverage to bring you closer to the action.
